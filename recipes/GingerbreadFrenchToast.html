Gingerbread French Toast

Make the night before, rolls rise in the fridge overnight and bake the next morning. Easy and delicious. Enjoy!

Warm gingerbread slices soak in a creamy vanilla custard, then transform into golden clouds of fluffy French toast. Each bite bursts with the cozy spices of winter, while the custard adds a touch of decadent sweetness. Drizzle with maple syrup or dust with powdered sugar for a festive finish that's sure to bring holiday cheer.
